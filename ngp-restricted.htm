Perhaps our silliest main category, New Game+ (Restricted) is fun, creative, tense, and most importantly, short for a Restricted run.

Combining our ruleset (not letting you skip or cancel most things) with a NG+ save file (where everything you need is maxed) leads to a familiar Restricted route that instead has you slinging bombs and lures left and right. You even get the lantern in this category, so you don't have to learn the dread lanternless routes.

Because the run is on the easier and shorter side, expect minor mistakes to lead to resets as you improve your PB.
