Double Admin Skip

Freeze both admins and power fuse before dying

  • Run about halfway to the fuse in the middle.
  • Turn toward and throw an ice bomb at each admin.
  • Activate the fuse.
  • Run back, wait 1-2 seconds, then run down and cross the bridge.
  • While waiting, switch out ice-bombs for your next equipment step.
If you aren't in critical HP, immediately after activating the fuse is a good chance to do it.
