Boss: Siege (Stunlock)

Use Orange Peril Ring to break poise and stunlock Siege Engine

  1. Make sure you are in peril state (Under 20% HP)
  2. Run to the Siege Engine and approach from the side.
  3. Roll to the middle just behind the center piece behind the face.
Phase 1: Waking Up

On first hit, the Siege will start to wake. For this phase:

  • If you are at ATT 1, use 1-2 combos and hit 8x to break poise.
  • If you are at ATT 4+, use 1-2 combos and hit 5x to break poise.
  • After the poise break, wait a beat before attacking again.
Phase 2: Stun-Lock

Once the boss staggers backwards, hold target and:

  • If you are at ATT 1: spam 1-2-3 combos to break poise. Continue this technique until the end of the fight.
  • If you are at ATT 4+: use 1-2 combos and hit 5x to break poise. Continue this technique until the end of the fight. After each poise break, wait a beat before attacking again. Otherwise you will swipe while the boss is recoiling, miss, and break out of the stun-lock.
  • Once the screen begins to fade from white, use this time to menu for the next step.
  • Obtain the Plinth Key and run to the plinth. Interact with it, select OK, and immediately pause/quit/continue.
